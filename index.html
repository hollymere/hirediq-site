The Problem

Why good people are not getting hired.

The job market is not broken because there are no opportunities. It is broken because the system is stacked against candidates who do not know the rules.

Your CV is being rejected before a human ever reads it.

Most companies use Applicant Tracking Systems that scan CVs for specific keywords and formatting. If your CV uses the wrong language or has tables and columns, it is filtered out automatically. You never even get a chance.

You are applying for roles you have no realistic chance of getting.

Without an honest assessment of your fit, you waste weeks applying for roles where you are a 30% match and wondering why no one responds. Every one of those applications is time you could have spent on roles where you genuinely stand out.

You are being eliminated in the screening call before you reach the hiring manager.

The screening call is not a formality. It is where most candidates are removed from the process. Recruiters are looking for specific things. Without preparation, you will say the wrong things or fail to address the gaps in your background.

You have no idea what is working and what is not.

Most job seekers apply, wait, hear nothing, and repeat the same process. No feedback. No data. No way of knowing if the problem is your CV, your targeting, your cover letter or your response rate. You are guessing in the dark.

Common Questions

Straight answers.

What makes HiredIQ different from Jobscan or Teal?

Jobscan matches keywords. Teal tracks applications. Neither was built by someone who has actually hired people at scale. HiredIQ combines honest matching, ATS optimisation, CV rewriting and screening preparation in one analysis. And unlike every competitor, we tell you when not to apply.

What is ATS optimisation and why does it matter?

Most companies use Applicant Tracking Systems to filter CVs before a human sees them. These systems scan for specific keywords and formatting. A CV that reads beautifully to a person can be invisible to an ATS. HiredIQ identifies every missing keyword and creates a plain text version of your CV that passes any ATS system.

How does the screening call preparation work?

Based on the job description and your CV, HiredIQ generates the 5 most likely questions a recruiter will ask you. For each question you get a suggested answer framework covering what to say, what to emphasise and how to address any gaps in your background before they become objections.

Is my CV data safe?

Yes. Your CV and job description are processed and then immediately deleted. Nothing is stored on our servers. We do not sell your data or use it to train AI models. Your email is only stored with your explicit consent.

Does it work for US job seekers as well as UK?

Yes. The analysis, ATS optimisation, CV rewriting and screening preparation all work for both markets. Salary context is provided in GBP for UK roles and USD for US roles. HiredIQ is built for global job seekers from day one.

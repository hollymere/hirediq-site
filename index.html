Common questions

What makes HiredIQ different from Jobscan or Teal?

Jobscan optimises keywords. Teal tracks applications. Neither gives you a genuinely honest assessment of whether you should apply for a role. HiredIQ was built by a former MD who has hired hundreds of people and has seen what actually gets candidates hired, and is prepared to tell you when you are wasting your time.

How does the match score work?

Our AI reads every requirement in the job description and assesses your CV against each one specifically. The score reflects real fit, not keyword density. A score below 50 means you have significant gaps. We tell you what they are and what to do about them.

Is my CV data safe?

Yes. Your CV and application data is never sold, shared or used to train AI models. It is used only to generate your analysis and is stored securely. You can delete your data at any time.

Does it work for US job seekers as well as UK?

Yes. The scoring, CV rewriting and cover letter generation work for both markets. Salary context is provided in GBP for UK roles and USD for US roles. We are built for global job seekers from day one.

What is the application tracker?

A dashboard where every application you make lives in one place. You can see which applications have gone cold, track your response rate by industry and role type, and get a weekly report on what is working. It turns a stressful invisible process into a manageable, measurable one.

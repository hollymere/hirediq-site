The Problem

Why good people are not getting hired.

The job market is not broken because there are no opportunities. It is broken because the system is stacked against candidates who do not know the rules.

Your CV is being rejected before a human ever reads it.

Most companies use Applicant Tracking Systems that scan CVs for specific keywords and formatting. If your CV uses the wrong language or has tables and columns, it is filtered out automatically. You never even get a chance.

You are applying for roles you have no realistic chance of getting.

Without an honest assessment of your fit, you waste weeks applying for roles where you are a 30% match and wondering why no one responds. Every one of those applications is time you could have spent on roles where you genuinely stand out.

Your CV is written for every role instead of this one.

A generic CV sent to every application is the single biggest reason good candidates get overlooked. The role you are applying for has specific language, specific requirements and specific priorities. Your CV needs to speak directly to all of them.

You have no idea what is working and what is not.

Most job seekers apply, wait, hear nothing, and repeat the same process. No feedback. No data. No way of knowing if the problem is your CV, your targeting or your approach. You are guessing in the dark.

Common Questions

Straight answers.

What makes HiredIQ different from Jobscan or Teal?

Jobscan matches keywords. Teal tracks applications. Neither was built by someone who has actually hired people at scale. HiredIQ gives you an honest verdict across four clear bands and rewrites your CV specifically for each role. And unlike every competitor, we tell you when not to apply.

Why do you not generate cover letters?

Because an AI cover letter works against you. Recruiters and hiring managers spot them immediately and they create a negative impression. A cover letter should answer three questions: why this role, why now, and why you. Those answers are personal. We tell you what to cover. You write it.

How does the CV rewrite work?

Once your score shows you are worth applying for the role, one click rewrites your CV for that specific role. Missing keywords are incorporated naturally, every section is tailored to what this employer is looking for, and it is formatted to pass any Applicant Tracking System automatically.

Is my CV data safe?

Yes. Your CV and job description are processed and then immediately deleted. Nothing is stored on our servers. We do not sell your data or use it to train AI models. Your email is only stored with your explicit consent.

Does it work for US job seekers as well as UK?

Yes. The scoring, CV rewriting and salary context all work for both markets. Salary context is provided in GBP for UK roles and USD for US roles. HiredIQ is built for global job seekers from day one.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes. No minimum term. No cancellation fees. Cancel anytime and you will not be charged again. If you get a job, cancel. Come back when you need it.
